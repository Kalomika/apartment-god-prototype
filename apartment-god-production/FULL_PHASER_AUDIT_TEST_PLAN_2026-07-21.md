4. Confirm one diagonal kitchen sink appears at the visible collision location. Tap around its edges and route a character to it.
5. Swipe between Main, Garage, Backyard, Front Yard, Secret Lab, and Driveway. Confirm one navigation response per gesture.
6. Background the browser for at least ten seconds, return, and confirm time did not advance through duplicate hidden intervals.
7. Restart the Phaser scene from a development preview, then repeat swipes and backgrounding. Confirm no duplicate listener behavior.
8. Load a version 2 save that lacks wardrobe, cleaning, careers, newer entities, or newer objects. Confirm saved money, positions, and needs remain while modern defaults are restored.
9. Send one resident offsite, then the whole visible household. Confirm remaining actors stay playable, all-away acceleration only occurs when intended, and return restores actor visibility, vehicle state, garage door state, and selection.
10. On Android, rotate portrait to landscape and back. Confirm the full 4:3 scene remains aligned and touch targets match visible objects.
