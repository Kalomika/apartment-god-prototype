# Room Style Guide

## Shared apartment style

The upgraded apartment should feel like a lived in cyberpunk home, not a show room and not a cute toy map. The visual target is realistic, readable, dark, and functional.

Shared rules:

- Top down orthographic clarity comes first.
- Dark wall masses define rooms.
- Blue grey floors unify the apartment.
- Cyan neon guides utility, circulation, and interaction.
- Magenta neon marks mood, entertainment, privacy, nightlife, or warning states.
- Clutter should tell the resident story but stay out of main walk paths.
- Furniture silhouettes should stay simple enough for small screen play.
- Every major prop must have a clear interaction side.

## Living room

Mood:

- Main social and entertainment hub.
- Dark, moody, neon lit, slightly messy.
- Feels used daily.

Palette:

- Dark charcoal walls.
- Blue grey floor panels.
- Cyan wall strip behind TV.
- Magenta accent near couch, stereo, or window.
- TV glow as a soft blue light pool.

Furniture:

- Couch as primary seating mass.
- TV and low console.
- Stereo or speaker stack.
- Optional coffee table future.
- Dog bowl nearby if current gameplay expects fast access.

Clutter:

- Cables near TV and stereo.
- Small food containers or cups at table edge.
- Game controller shapes, data chips, remote, old shoes.
- Keep clutter against prop edges, not in walk lanes.

Gameplay objects:

- Couch
- TV
- Stereo
- Dog bowl
- Living light
- Window future mood support

Lighting:

- TV glow state.
- Cyan trim along entertainment wall.
- Magenta music state pulses from stereo side.
- Room light can shift from on to dim to off.

Cyberpunk details:

- Wall mounted holo panel frame.
- Speaker LEDs.
- Exposed cable raceway.
- City glow in window glass.

## Kitchen

Mood:

- Compact utility space for hunger and cooking.
- Functional, hard edged, slightly worn.

Palette:

- Dark slate floor.
- Blue grey counters.
- Cyan under cabinet strips.
- Magenta appliance indicator lights.
- Orange or blue stove glow when active.

Furniture:

- Fridge.
- Stove.
- Sink.
- Counter run.
- Cabinet blocks.
- Future dining table, placed near kitchen but not blocking movement.

Clutter:

- Dishes, pans, food packs, small bottles.
- Keep props low contrast unless they are interactable.
- Use one or two readable clusters, not scattered noise.

Gameplay objects:

- Fridge
- Stove
- Sink
- Future dining table

Lighting:

- Under cabinet cyan strip.
- Stove glow when cooking.
- Overhead kitchen panel for lights on state.
- Dim state leaves appliance LEDs visible.

Cyberpunk details:

- Smart fridge panel.
- Induction stove glow.
- Counter embedded light seam.
- Compact recycler future.
