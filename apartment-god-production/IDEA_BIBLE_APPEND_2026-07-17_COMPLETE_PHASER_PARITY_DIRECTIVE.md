The Phaser version is not a separate reduced game or prototype. It must be the complete current Apartment God game with the same working house, objects, activities, UI, phone, saves, autonomy, movement, pathfinding, exterior spaces, vehicles, arcade games, basketball, pool, offsite continuity, relationships, career systems, dog systems, and corrections.

The engine and rendering architecture may change, but working content and functionality must not disappear during migration.

Required visual direction:

- Persistent true top down character sprites, not bathroom sign figures, blobs, mascots, or generic symbols.
- Resident, Girlfriend, Lab Test Subject, Dog, and later actors must use grounded full body directional sprites.
- Directional movement animation must play at 8 FPS.
- Walk animation must require real coordinate movement. Running in place is rejected.
- Every major activity requires its own entry, loop, exit, object alignment, direction needs, fallback, and test plan.
- Pool players must physically circulate around the pool table, stop at a specific stance, align hands and cue, take the shot, watch the result, and change stations between turns.

Promotion rule:

Do not replace main with the Phaser branch until feature parity, boot, build, mobile input, sprite scale, activity routing, and browser behavior have been tested. Preserve main and migration backups before every promotion attempt.
