# Browser Test Checklist

This branch touches runtime systems across routing, actions, delivery, objects, and floors.

Before merging to main, test:

- App loads without JS errors.
- Floor buttons work.
- Basement renders.
- Characters can move on each floor.
- Stairs and basement stairs work.
- Phone food delivery shows a courier and completes.
- Fridge snack opens visibly and restores hunger after action.
- Cooking starts from upstairs and routes to the fridge/stove.
- Pool, arcade, console, and darts actions start.
- Together actions can be accepted or refused.

Merge after this manual pass or after a successful automated check/build run.
